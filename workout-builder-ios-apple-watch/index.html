Workout Builder for iPhone and Apple Watch

Create custom workouts on your iPhone and send them to your Apple Watch

How do I build custom workouts for Apple Watch on my iPhone?

Download Workout Builder to create your own workouts for Apple Watch.

Workout Builder is a free app available on iOS 17 and watchOS 10.

Use Workout Builder to create your own workouts to send to your Apple Watch to run via the Workouts app.

Create your own workouts from scratch for free or use our premium pre-built workouts to perform advanced workouts on your Apple Watch.

Does Workout Builder come with any pre-built workouts?

Yes! Workout Builder comes with great premium workouts for many of the most popular sports.

  • Running workouts for tempo runs, intervals, hill sprints
  • Cycling workouts for intervals, pyramids, and distance efforts
  • Swimming workouts for specific distances and times
  • Flow, Yin, Vinyasa yoga
  • HIIT & Strength training intervals
  • Boxing and skipping intervals, with rest included
  • Plus many, many more sports!

What types of workouts can Workout Buidler create?

Workout Builder has three types of workouts:

  • Simple, goal-based workouts – set a time, distance, or energy burn goal
  • Advanced interval workouts – add multiple sets and reps, along with rest, warmup, and cooldown
  • Paced workouts – set a time and distance goal and pace your workout
