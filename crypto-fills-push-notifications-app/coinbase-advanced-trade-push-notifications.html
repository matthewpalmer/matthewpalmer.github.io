How to receive push notifications for trades and order fills on Coinbase Advanced Trade

1. Download the Crypto Fills iOS app and complete the "Get Started" step, giving permission for the app to send push notifications

2. Tap the '+' button in the top right, then select the Coinbase Pro option

3. In Coinbase Advanced Trade, generate read-only API keys in your account settings

4. Copy and paste your API key and secret into the Crypto Fills app

5. Tap "Test", and when it succeeds, tap "Save"
