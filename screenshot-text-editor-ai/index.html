How to Edit Text in a Screenshot with AI

Getting started with Screenshot Text Editor AI is simple and straightforward. Just follow these steps:

  • Step 1: Add your screenshot. Upload or drag and drop your image into the Screenshot Text Editor AI interface.
  • Step 2: Select or auto-detect regions to overwrite. You can manually highlight areas you want to edit, or let the app automatically detect text regions.
  • Step 3: Enter text or generate content to fill in the spaces. Customize your screenshot with new text or use AI-generated content that fits your needs.
  • Step 4: Save the cleaned-up image. Once you’re satisfied with the edits, simply save your image and it’s ready to use.
