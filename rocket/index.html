Rocket is a free Mac app that makes typing emoji faster and easier using Slack-style shortcuts.

Just start typing an emoji like :thumbsup: (👍) and Rocket will help auto-complete it – in any app!

Plus, with Rocket Pro, you can add gifs, snippets, and symbols to your Rocket shortcuts.
