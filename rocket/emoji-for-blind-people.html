Rocket vs Rocket Pro

In the free version of Rocket, you get:

  • A Slack-style emoji picker with VoiceOver support for your Mac
  • Access to every emoji through the shortcut window
  • To pick your preferred skin tone
  • Light and dark themes

Rocket Pro is a $10 in-app upgrade that gives you the ability to:

  • Perform full-text search on all the emoji and assign custom shortcuts
  • Use Rocket to send gifs, images, and memes
  • Add your own emoji
